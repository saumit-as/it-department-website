INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Information technology is the use of computers to store or retrieve dataand information.IT is typically used within the context of business operations as opposed to personal or entertainment technologies. IT is considered to be a subset of information and communications technology. An information technology system is generally an information, a communication system,or,more system,specifically speaking, a computer system – including all hardware, software, and peripheral equipment – operated by a limited group of IT users.
